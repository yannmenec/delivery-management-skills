# Escalation Memo Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to score outputs from `craft-escalation-memo`.

## Scoring (0-2 per dimension, 10 total)

### 1. Situation Clarity (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | The situation is described in 2-3 sentences with specific facts: what is happening, since when, which tickets/teams are involved |
| 1 | Situation is described but vague or too long |
| 0 | Situation is unclear or missing |

### 2. Impact Quantification (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Impact is quantified: story points at risk, number of teams affected, days of delay, revenue/user impact if applicable |
| 1 | Impact is described qualitatively but not quantified |
| 0 | Impact is missing or vague ("this is bad") |

### 3. Urgency and Deadline (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Clear deadline stated with consequence of missing it. Urgency is proportional to the actual risk. |
| 1 | Deadline exists but consequence is unclear, or urgency is inflated/understated |
| 0 | No deadline, or urgency framing is missing |

### 4. Specific Ask (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | The ask is specific: exactly what decision or action is needed, from whom, by when |
| 1 | Ask exists but is vague ("we need help") or directed at no one specific |
| 0 | No clear ask, or multiple conflicting asks |

### 5. Professional Tone (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Tone is factual, urgent without panic, blame-free, and solution-oriented |
| 1 | Mostly professional but with traces of blame, frustration, or passive aggression |
| 0 | Emotional, blaming, or unprofessional |

## Verdict

| Total | Verdict |
|-------|---------|
| 8-10 | **Pass** — ready to send |
| 5-7 | **Iterate** — adjust tone or add specificity |
| 0-4 | **Fail** — rewrite needed |
