# Sprint Report Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to score sprint reports generated by `generate-sprint-report` or `sprint-health-check`.

## Scoring (0-2 per dimension, 12 total)

### 1. RAG Accuracy (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | RAG status matches the data: Green with >80% trajectory and ≤1 blocker; Amber with 60-80% or 2+ blockers; Red with <60% or critical blockers |
| 1 | RAG is defensible but borderline (e.g., Amber when data supports either Green or Amber) |
| 0 | RAG contradicts the data (e.g., Green with 3 critical blockers and declining velocity) |

### 2. Velocity Accuracy (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Velocity numbers match source data. Trend direction is correct. Predictability score is mathematically sound. |
| 1 | Velocity is approximately correct (within 5% margin) or trend direction is right but magnitude is wrong |
| 0 | Velocity numbers are fabricated or materially wrong |

### 3. Blocker Specificity (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Every blocker references a specific ticket key, has a description, and includes days stuck and assignee |
| 1 | Blockers are listed but missing detail (no days stuck, or generic descriptions) |
| 0 | Blockers are vague ("some tickets are blocked") or fabricated |

### 4. Scope Change Accuracy (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Scope change numbers match: correct count of added/removed tickets, correct SP delta, accurate percentage |
| 1 | Direction is right but numbers are approximate |
| 0 | Scope change missed entirely, or numbers are fabricated |

### 5. Recommendation Quality (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | Recommendations are specific (reference ticket keys, names, dates), actionable (clear next step), and tied to the data |
| 1 | Recommendations are reasonable but generic ("follow up on blockers") |
| 0 | No recommendations, or recommendations are irrelevant to the data |

### 6. Completeness and Format (0-2)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 2 | All required sections present: Header/RAG, Summary, Progress, Velocity, Blockers, Scope Changes, Recommendations, Confidence |
| 1 | Most sections present but 1-2 are missing or very thin |
| 0 | Multiple sections missing or output is unstructured |

## Verdict

| Total | Verdict |
|-------|---------|
| 10-12 | **Pass** — ready to share |
| 7-9 | **Iterate** — fix identified gaps |
| 0-6 | **Fail** — requires significant revision |
